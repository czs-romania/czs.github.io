1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how COZAC-SYSTEMS SRL ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the DIAGO mobile application ("the App").

DIAGO is a school bus tracking application that allows parents to monitor bus locations in real-time and receive notifications about their children's boarding and disembarking activities.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: Verify your identity and secure your account
  • Real-time Tracking: Display bus locations on the map
  • Notifications: Alert you when the bus is near your station or when your child boards/exits
  • Student Safety: Track boarding and disembarking events
  • Service Improvement: Analyze bus routes and timing (using aggregated, non-personal data)

5. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We share data with the following third-party service providers:

5.1 Cloudflare R2 (Cloud Storage)

  • Purpose: Store historical bus location data for reporting
  • Data Shared: Bus GPS coordinates and timestamps only
  • No personal user data is shared

5.2 Expo Push Notification Service

  • Purpose: Deliver push notifications to your device
  • Data Shared: Device push token, notification content
  • Provider: Expo (https://expo.dev)

5.3 Google Maps

  • Purpose: Display maps and calculate estimated arrival times
  • Data Shared: Bus coordinates, station coordinates
  • No personal user data is shared

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time for consent-based processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at contact@czs.ro.

We will respond to your request within 30 days. If we cannot fulfill your request, we will explain why.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted Storage: Authentication tokens are stored in encrypted device storage (Expo Secure Store)
  • Secure Transmission: All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
  • Access Control: Database access is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Token-Based Authentication: Secure JWT tokens with expiration
  • No Password Storage: We use access codes, not passwords

9. Children's Privacy

DIAGO is designed to track school buses and students. We recognize that some users may be minors.

  • Parental Consent: Student accounts are created and managed by parents or school administrators
  • Minimal Data: We collect only the minimum data necessary (alias and optional beacon)
  • No Direct Collection from Children: Children do not directly provide personal information to the App

If you believe we have collected personal information from a child without proper consent, please contact us immediately at contact@czs.ro.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily processed within the European Union. When data is transferred to third-party services (such as push notification providers), we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses where applicable.
